Casa Do Leão

The “Casa do Leão” is the ideal place for a peaceful and luxurious stay in Madeira. Located on a Hillside in Fajã da Ovelha, you will be able to observe the Atlantic Ocean as well as the sunset all throughout the house, mainly on it’s three balconies or even from the garden. It accommodates five people, with three bedrooms, two with a couple’s bed, and one with a single’s bed and an extra bed can be added. The living room and kitchen are spacious and modern. Features free Wi-Fi and Cable TV.

Paradise in Prazeres

Paradise in Prazeres is a secluded vacation house, perfect for a peaceful stay with mountain views and forest surrounding the house it provides an enjoyable and relaxing experience. One of the main features of this house, is it’s heated pool all year long! It is a three-bedroom house with two bathrooms, one living room, a kitchen and dining area. Free Wifi and Cable Tv is featured.

Apartamento Jardim do Mar

Located in Jardim do Mar, this apartment features free WiFi and a balcony. The property boasts views of the sea, it is located 30 km from Funchal. There is a seating area and a kitchen complete with a microwave, a fridge and a stovetop. A flat-screen TV is featured.

Casa Jardim do Mar Sea View

Casa da Ladeira

Casa da Ladeira is a detached holiday home with a year-round outdoor pool, set in Fajã do Pereira. Guests benefit from terrace, a wonderful view and free private parking is available on site.

Apartament Ladeira Centro

Apartament Ladeira Centro offers accommodation in Funchal. Apartament Ladeira Centro boasts views of the sea and is 900m from Marina do Funchal. Free WiFi is available throughout the property.
