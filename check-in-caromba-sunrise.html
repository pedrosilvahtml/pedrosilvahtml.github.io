Jacuzzi

Maintaining a jacuzzi requires considerable care and attention. While we are committed to providing necessary assistance, we also count on you, our guest, to help us keep the jacuzzi in working condition.
Your cooperation ensures both your enjoyment and the continued experience of future guests.



Rules
  • Always keep the jacuzzi covered when not in use.
  • Shower before use.
  • Use appropriate swimwear.
  • Enter and exit with care.
  • No pushing or jumping into the jacuzzi.
  • Avoid using creams, oils or other body products.
  • No food, utensils or glass items.
  • Be mindful of children around the jacuzzi.
Usage
  • Never use the filter operation cycle at the same time as using bubbles, or while using the jacuzzi, the filter cycle is only meant to be turned on after usage.
  • You should expect the jacuzzi to NOT be heated when you arrive, if you plan on using the jacuzzi during your stay, please turn on the heating cycle and allow some time (8-12h) depending on outside temperature, to heat up with the cover closed, detailed instructions below.
  • After using the jacuzzi please turn on the filter operating cycle, and allow the cycle to run for 4 to 8 hours. It then has to be manually turned off. (view image below)
Operation and Troubleshooting
  • In regular usage, please only use the bubbles, temperature and filter buttons, unless instructed otherwise.

Check-out information

Close all doors and windows, return all keys to the lockbox used for your check-in, the code remains the same.
After closing the lockbox, please change the code numbers and close the cover to ensure it remains confidential once you have left.

The check-out time limit is at noon.

Guests are expected to maintain a standard level of cleanliness during their stay and upon check-out. Additional fees may apply if:

  • Trash is not properly disposed of.
  • Items such as bedding, towels, kitchenware, or other household items are damaged beyond normal use.
  • Smoking occurs inside the house.
  • Extensive cleaning required beyond what is expected.
  • Windows, doors, gates and lights left open/on.
  • Keys not returned.

We appreciate your cooperation!
